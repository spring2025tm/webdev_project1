The Ultimate Hobby

Below I have selected a few of my favorites from my current houseplant collection. It is a very calming pastime I picked up during the 2020 pandemic. It is the perfect mix of calming, low-effort, and challenging to that make it a great activity.

I love to take on new species and learn what makes them very happy or very, very sad. I have, unfortunately, killed a handful of plants in the name of experimentation and learning.

However, its important to note that I have learned a lot from my succeess and my failures. I now have a solid, growing understanding of indoor and outdoor plant care.

  • Begonia Rex "Silver"
  • Monstera Cobra
  • White Wizrd Philodendron
