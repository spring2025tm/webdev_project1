My Plants

Below I have selected a few of my favority houseplants in my current collection. It is a very calming passtime I picked up during the 2020 pandemic. It is the perfect mix of calming, low-effort, and challenging to that make it a great activity.

I love to take on new species and learn what makes them very happy or very, very sad. I have killed a handful of plants in the name of experimentation.

However, its important to note that I learned the most from my failed care attempts and now have a solid, growing understanding of indoor and outdoor plant care.
