Hey there!

My name is Tyler. I am a third-year transfer student pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Information Technology Management here at the Institute.

It feels great to be in a community that is thriving, diverse, and brimming with opportunity. My Associate's degree is in Cybersecurity with a certificate in Advanced Cybersecurity.

My interest in cybersecurity continues to grow as I continue to be exposed to all of its different faces.

I have been fortunate enough to witness the power of information security in academia and in corporate environments. I really enjoyed a short stint in the Identity and Access Management realm. Most recently, I have been learning in the Security Awareness and Training space.

Otherwise known as Human Risk, Security Awareness is just one of the underrated opportunities in cybersecurity. Information security, governance, and regulation are all fun. However, I ultimately appreciate the versatility in tools and skills used in the security space. Kind of like the HTML, CSS, and web hygiene skills we are learning in this class!
